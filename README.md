# podosome_dynamics
MATLAB is required to run the code. All scripts can be executed directly.

life_cycle.m: Plots the quantitative time course of key variables over the oscillatory cycle.

phase_diagram.m: Generates a three-dimensional phase diagram mapping the rich dynamical landscape of podosome behavior across varying mechanical and biochemical conditions.

biochemical_noise.m: Analyzes the impact of biochemical noise on podosome oscillatory behavior and plots the coefficient of variation (CV) of the oscillation period as a function of noise intensity.

coherence_resonance.m: Investigates the effect of noise on the phase boundary, demonstrating noise-induced coherence resonance.

stability_analysis_scaling_law.m: Evaluates the statistical robustness of the mechanosensing scaling law.

asynchronous_synchronous.m: Illustrates the spatiotemporal evolution of podosome arrays driven by noise.

fisher.m: Calculates the Fisher information to demonstrate the precision of podosome mechanosensing on substrates of varying stiffness.

waveform_encoding.m: Reveals how podosome oscillations encode substrate stiffness through both waveform shape and proximity to the Hopf bifurcation.

waveform_criticality_efficiency.m: Explores how the mechanical environment influences podosome communication efficiency.

p_myosin_stiffness.m: Demonstrates the effect of p-myosin on podosome oscillatory dynamics.

effect_spacing.m: Highlights how strong diffusion-mediated coupling can disrupt the single-oscillator scaling law when podosomes are closely spaced.

Note: For all the scripts listed above, you are encouraged to tune the parameters to explore the underlying biophysics.
